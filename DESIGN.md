#DESIGN.md

## 🎯 Design Philosophy

Clean, modern, and premium.
The UI must feel intentional, spacious, and professional—not generic or template-like.

---

## 🎨 Color System

Primary:

* #1F5F4A (Deep Green)

Secondary:

* #CFF5D2 (Soft Green)

Accent (CTA):

* #6EE7A8 (Bright Green)

Background:

* #F7F9F8 (Off White)

Text:

* Primary: #1A1A1A
* Secondary: #6B7280

---

## 🔤 Typography

Font Family:

* Headings: Sora / Poppins / Inter
* Body: Inter / Open Sans

Scale:

* H1: 48–64px (bold)
* H2: 32–40px
* H3: 24–28px
* Body: 16–18px

Line Height:

* Comfortable (1.5–1.7)

---

## 📐 Spacing System

Use 8px scale:

* xs: 8px
* sm: 16px
* md: 24px
* lg: 32px
* xl: 48px
* 2xl: 64px

Section padding:

* py-16 or py-24

Container:

* max-w-6xl or max-w-7xl
* centered (mx-auto px-4)

---

## 🧱 Layout Rules

* Use grid or flex layouts
* Maintain consistent alignment
* Avoid clutter
* Ensure visual hierarchy

---

## 🧩 Components

### Buttons

* Rounded-xl
* Primary: green background
* Secondary: outline
* Hover: subtle scale + color shift

---

### Cards

* Rounded-2xl
* Soft shadow
* Padding: p-6 or p-8
* Hover: slight lift

---

### Sections

* Clear separation
* Consistent spacing
* Optional background variation

---

## ⚡ Animations

* Smooth and subtle only
* Fade-in on scroll
* Hover transitions
* No excessive motion

---

## 🚫 Avoid

* Generic layouts
* Tight spacing
* Harsh shadows
* Inconsistent styles
* Overuse of colors

---

## 🧠 Visual Standard

Every section must:

* Look balanced
* Have breathing space
* Feel intentional
