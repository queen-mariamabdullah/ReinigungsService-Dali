# PRODUCT.md

## 🧾 Product Overview

A premium, modern cleaning services website for a Germany-based business.
The goal is to present services clearly, build trust, and convert visitors into leads.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Communicate professionalism and reliability
* Showcase cleaning services clearly
* Encourage users to contact or book
* Provide a smooth and simple user experience

---

## 👥 Target Users

* Homeowners
* Office managers
* Property managers
* Busy professionals

---

## 🧱 Pages & Requirements

### 1. Home Page

**Goal:** Convert visitors into leads

Sections:

* Hero (headline, subtext, CTA)
* Services overview (grid/cards)
* Why Choose Us (trust points)
* Testimonials
* Blog preview (optional)
* Call-to-action section

---

### 2. About Page

**Goal:** Build trust

Sections:

* Company introduction
* Mission & values
* Professional positioning

---

### 3. Services Page

**Goal:** Explain offerings clearly

Sections:

* Home cleaning
* Office cleaning
* Deep cleaning
* Optional pricing/packages

---

### 4. Contact Page

**Goal:** Capture leads

Features:

* Contact form (name, email, message)
* Business contact info
* Google Maps embed (Germany location)

---

## ⚡ User Experience Requirements

* Mobile-first design
* Fast loading
* Clear navigation
* Strong call-to-actions
* Smooth interactions

---

## 🚫 Non-Goals

* No complex booking system (MVP only)
* No backend-heavy features

---

## 📊 Success Criteria

* Professional UI
* Fully responsive
* Easy navigation
* Functional contact form

---

## 🧠 Key Principle

The website must feel like a **real business product**, not a template or beginner project.
